The Ann Arbor Area Board of REALTORS® Good Neightbor Award recognizes REALTOR® members who have positively impacted their community and organizations through which they volunteer. This annual award seeks to highlight, inspire, and perpetuate the individual contributions within the real estate industry toward improving the livability of our communities as well as supporting the local non-profits that exist to do the same.
